About Design

  • Common misconception that design is about appearance and aesthetics
  • Design is a term that is open to interpretation, and there is not only one way to look at it, however
    • Functionality, experience, structure etc are all things that we design
    • Design processes allow us to consider and test alternatives so that we, as best as we can ensure better outcomes
  • In particular, Studio 3 does not have a Design part and an Implementation part. Proposal and Build are both concerned with design, but with the emphasis on what, and then how.
  • Whilst aesthetics are a key part of things that you design, they are more-so an indicator of the attention that you pay to what you are designing and how your users will interact with it
  • Whilst your design may be technologically brilliant, they need to be usable by your intended users.
  • Wicked Problem A problem that is difficult or impossible to solve, with no defined end

Project Phases

    • Divergent approach to design rather that leaping onto solutions early

      • As opposed to convergent solutions
